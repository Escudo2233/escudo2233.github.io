About Me


Hello! My name is Escudo (or at least I go by Escudo online), and I made this website. Pretty neat huh? I guess I should add more pertinent information about myself though. Were to begin?

Well for starters, I'm a 19-year-old Computer Science student who lives in Portugal (specifically, the Azores). I'm Portuguese-Canadian, and I like to draw stuff, mostly maps.

I'm pretty well-versed in both Portuguese and English, though I'm understandably more well-read on the former. I enjoy reading the Portuguese literature classics, but overall I'm mostly a sucker for a good fantasy book (read the Mistborn trilogy). If I'm not reading or drawing, I'm probably playing something. From TTRPGs to their videogame counterparts. I also have a passing interest in woodworking and the kitchen.

I'm an avid collector of knowledge. I enjoy learning a lot of things in a lot of different areas, but I wouldn't have taken up Computer Science if I didn't have a particular fondness for computers. I still somewhat naively hope to make a game someday.

What's with the profile picture thing? Is that a coin? Yes, it is. "Escudo" is the name of the currency Portugal used before the Euro was a thing, and it means "Shield". I've used it as my profile picture in a lot of places, it's become sort of my thing. Can't really explain why, I just think it looks neat.

So there you have it, a brief explanation of what goes on inside this head.
